About Me

As a curious and multidisciplinary problem-solver, I'm driven to push the boundaries of what's possible with physics. With extensive software development experience, a background in audio engineering, and working on a Master's degree in Complex Systems, I bring a unique perspective to technical challenges. My passion for finding solutions to complex problems has led to a range of exciting research experiences, including studying superspreader events in the COVID-19 crisis, the universality of prediction markets, the impacts of youth programming on developmental outcomes, and the concealment of network information in deep learning. If you're looking for a creative and ambitious problem-solver to tackle your toughest challenges, I'm your candidate.
